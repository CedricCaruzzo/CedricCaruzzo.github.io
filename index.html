About

I am an AI researcher working at the intersection of computer vision, self-supervised learning, and foundation models. My research focuses on building robust and generalizable AI systems for oncology, with the long-term goal of enabling clinically impactful applications.

Before joining Lunit, I worked on Cell Painting at Institut Pasteur Korea, developing large-scale models for drug discovery. I also completed my MSc at KAIST AI under Prof. Jong Chul Ye, where I proposed new approaches for foundation models in Cell Painting and multimodal translation between cell morphology and chemistry.
